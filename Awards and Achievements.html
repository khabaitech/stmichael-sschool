Awards & Achievements

Each category has eight awards for a student’s or group’s significant contribution in the assigned areas. Each award is a gift of the interest on Rs. 5000/-.



1.Promotion of Literacy Award: Literacy campaigns, educating poor children, conducting non-formal & informal classes.

2.Environment Conciousness Award: Anti-pollution campaigns, ecological concerns, environment protection, planting trees etc


3.Social Awareness Award : Campaign for human rights. Fearless action for justice and social evils, anti-dowry campaigns etc.


4.Creative Life Award : Creativity in constructive work conscientisation and opinion mobilisation against drugs & alcoholism, community health programmes, drinking water and sanitation programmes, preventive medicine, etc.

5.National Intergration Award: Appreciation of pluralistic faiths and lifestyles, campaigns against communalism and religious fundamentalism, action for peace and unity etc.

6.Promotion of Indian Culture Award : Indian art, music, dance, cultural festivals, promotion of the heritage of Bihar etc.


7.Patna Beautification Award : Community awareness and mobilisation for cleaning and beautifying one’s locality with a follow-up system for the maintenance of the same.


8. Promotion of Science Awareness : Creating awareness of the importance of science in our daily life, projects benefitting rural development, indigenous technology and creating a scientific temper and awareness of the environment through science.
